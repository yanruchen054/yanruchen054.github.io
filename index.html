About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Electronic and Computer Engineering at UC San Diego, advised by Prof. Tajana Rosing in the Systems Energy Efficiency Lab (SEELab). My research focuses on heterogeneous processing-in-memory (PIM) architectures and system co-design for efficient AI and graph reasoning.

I build full-stack solutions from algorithm scheduling to cycle-level simulation and hardware mapping, targeting high throughput and energy efficiency.

Outside research, I enjoy singing.
